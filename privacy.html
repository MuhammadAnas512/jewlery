Privacy Policy

At Eterna Jewels, your privacy is treated with the same care and respect as our finest creations. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency in how it is used

Information We Collect

When you interact with our website, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, contact details, shipping address, and payment-related information. This information is collected only when necessary to provide you with a seamless shopping experience.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

✦ Process and fulfill your orders

✦ Communicate order updates and customer support responses

✦ Improve our website, services, and overall customer experience

✦ Send exclusive updates or offers (only if you choose to receive them)

Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal information. Your data is protected against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Sharing of Information

Eterna Jewels does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information is shared only with trusted service partners when required to complete transactions or deliver services, and always under strict confidentiality.
